My Steps

This is a personal recipe, not one borrowed

  1. Combine your dry ingredients in a seperate bowl
  2. Combine your sugars, butter, and flavouring in another bowl
  3. Add 2 eggs, one at a time, to the bowl of sugars and etc
  4. Slowly incorporate your bowl of mixed dry ingredients to your bowl of wet ingredients, whislt the mixer is running. DO THIS AT A LOW SPEED
  5. Using a spatula, mix your white chocolate chips into the batter. Do it slowly so you can make sure that you do not over-mix the batter
  6. Spoon the batter into your 9x9 baking pan. Make sure the pan has some protectant on it
  7. While the batter is in the oven for 20-23 minutes, in a seperate bowl, combine your unsalted butter and cream cheese until smooth/softer than a cloud
  8. Add in a small amount of vanilla flavouring
  9. Now that the bar pan is out of the oven, wait for it to cool down, then top it off with your frosting and extra white chocolate chips
